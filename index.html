Redefining Waste: Circular Textile Waste Map

This systemic design mapping captures the complex circular value chain of imported and domestic textile waste processing in the **Kandla Special Economic Zone (KASEZ)** in Gujarat, India.

Giga-mapping is a tool used in systemic design to visualize highly complex socio-technical networks, showing physical material flows, policy dependencies, environmental emissions, and human worker schedules simultaneously.

Map System Legend
  • Green Paths: Recycled material flows / circular loops
  • Blue Nodes: Technical infrastructure & machinery
  • Red Values: Environmental carbon & particulate emissions
  • Amber Outlines: Policy agencies & regulatory frameworks
  • Violet Texts: Worker activity & narrative scripts
